I am a Master's student in Computer Science at the **University of Southern California (USC)**, where I work under the **SAIL Lab** working with **Prof. Shrikanth S. Narayanan**.

Before joining USC, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at **Chulalongkorn University**, where I graduated with first-class honors (GPA 3.92). I also worked as a research assistant under **Asst. Prof. Ekapol Chuangsuwanich**. My work included speech enhancement for real-time applications, anti-spoofing, and a dentist voice assistant system.
